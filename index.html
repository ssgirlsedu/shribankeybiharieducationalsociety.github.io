Gaushala

The sheltering of old, abandoned, unproductive, infertile and infirm cows in shelters, referred to as “Gaushalas” is a traditional practice in India.

Vridha Ashram

Place where elderly people live and receive care when their old age makes it impossible for them to care for themselves at home

Education

Shri Bankey Bihari Welfare Society is an organization which is created for innovative learning to improve the quality of education in India.

Our Objectives

Mission & Vision

The objective of the organization is to make poor, helpless and handicapped children self-employed and self-dependent by implementing various schemes run by the government like free sewing, embroidery, spinning, knitting, handicrafts, computer typing etc.

To make efforts for educational maintenance of helpless children and to make them capable and to establish free health centers, children's home, old age home and educational institutes.
